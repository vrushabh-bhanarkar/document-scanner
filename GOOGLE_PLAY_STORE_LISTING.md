## ADDITIONAL REQUIRED FIELDS

Before publishing, complete these in Play Console:

### Content Rating
- Go to: **App content > Content ratings**
- Complete the questionnaire
- Expected rating: PEGI 3 (this is a utility app)

### Privacy Policy
- Go to: **App content > Privacy policy**
- Link to your complete privacy policy URL
- REQUIRED by Google Play

### Ads Declaration  
- Go to: **App content > Ads**
- Answer YES if app contains Google Mobile Ads
- App will be labeled "Contains ads"

### Target Audience
- Go to: **App content > Target audience and content**
- Select: NOT specifically for children
- Age group: Adults and all ages

- Show: Scanner, PDF Editor, OCR, File Management, Results
